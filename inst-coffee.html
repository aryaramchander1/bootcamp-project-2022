Instant Coffee

"This instant coffee takes less than 5 minutes to make, and tastes just as refreshing as any other coffee! And, it's the perfect compliment to your banana bread!"

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of milk, preferably 2% or whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons coffee powder

Instructions

  1. Heat up the milk for about 45 seconds in a microwave.
  2. Add the coffee mix and the sugar and stir completely.
  3. Microwave the milk, powder, and sugar for another 30 seconds and stir when finished.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

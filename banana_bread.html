Eggless Banana Bread

"This eggless dish uses common household ingredients to make the best banana bread to ever exist! And, you'll never guess that it's eggless!"

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe/overripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup raw sugar
  • 1/2 cup sunflower oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 2/3 pinches of grated nutmeg (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla powder
  • 1.5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Put the bananas and sugar in a mixing bowl and mash/puree them.
  2. Add the oil, vanilla, cinnamon powder, and nutmeg powder and mix thoroughly.
  3. In another bowl, sieve the whole weat flower, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Add to the banana puree.
  4. Fold the dry and wet ingredients in a cut-and-fold method. Do not mix.
  5. If the batter looks very thick or dry, mix about 1/3 or 1/4 cup of water and fold the batter again.
  6. Pour the mixture into the bread pan.
  7. Bake at 180 degrees Celsius or 350 degrees Fahrenheit for about 30-40 minutes. A toothpick or bamboo skewer inserted into the bread should come out clean - this means the bread is fully cooked.
  8. Let cool, slice, serve, and enjoy!
